How to Use
This Syllabus

Don't just bookmark this page and forget it. Here's a simple framework to turn these channels into real skills over summer.

01

Pick Your Focus Area

Choose 1–2 categories that excite you most. Don't try to watch everything — depth beats breadth every time.

02

Watch 3 Videos/Week

Commit to just 3 videos per week minimum. That's one hour of intentional learning — something most students won't do.

03

Apply What You Learn

Every video should trigger one action. Start a project, write a note, try a tool. Learning without doing is just entertainment.

04

Follow FreshMinds

Join our Telegram and YouTube for weekly drops of resources, tips, and community challenges designed specifically for freshmen.
